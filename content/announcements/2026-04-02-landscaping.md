Spring is here, and our landscaping crews will begin seasonal maintenance the
week of **April 13**. Expect mowing, mulching, and tree-trimming throughout the
common areas.

### Volunteer Cleanup Day — Saturday, April 18

Join your neighbors for our annual common-area cleanup from **9:00 AM to noon**.
We'll provide gloves, bags, and refreshments. Meet at the clubhouse parking lot.

If you have landscaping concerns about your property line or a common area,
please reach out through the [contact form](/contact).
