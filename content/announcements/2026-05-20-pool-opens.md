We're excited to announce that the **community pool** will open for the 2026
season on **Saturday, May 23** at 10:00 AM!

## What's new this year

- Extended weekend hours: 8:00 AM – 9:00 PM on Saturdays and Sundays
- A refreshed shaded seating area near the shallow end
- Swim lessons for kids ages 4–10 (sign up at the clubhouse)

## Pool access

All residents in good standing receive a key fob for pool access. If you need a
new or replacement fob, stop by the clubhouse during office hours. Please
remember that **guests must be accompanied by a resident** at all times.

Let's have a safe and fun summer together!
